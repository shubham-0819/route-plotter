How Route Plotting Works

Level 1 Routes

Continued Trips: When destinations are connected (e.g., Bangalore → Chennai, Chennai → Ooty), the routes are plotted as straight lines on Level 1.

Non-Continued Trips: When destinations are not connected (e.g., Bangalore → Chennai, Ooty → Bangalore), the routes are plotted with arrows on Level 1.

Level 2 Routes

When consecutive trips have the same pickup and drop location, they are plotted on Level 2 to avoid overlapping with other routes.

Example Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Bangalore → Chennai, Chennai → Ooty (Level 1, connected)
  • Scenario 2: Bangalore → Chennai, Ooty → Bangalore (Level 1, with arrows)
  • Scenario 3: Bangalore → Chennai, Bangalore → Chennai (Level 2)
